History

It was the dream of Dr.Kurunji Venkatramana Gowda that English Education must be accessible to the inquisitive minds of Sullia and its neighbouring places.
It was in the year 2008 that Dr.Renuka Prasad K.V felt the need for a CBSE school in this serene land. His desire was fostered by his belief that “Children must be taught how to think, not what to think”. Dr.Jyothi R.Prasad joined hands with him in his mission. The school started with a humble beginning. As the years passed by the school earned good name and fame. The strength of the school today is its dynamic faculty who could make the students to think. Within a short span of time the school is recognized globally. Some of its accomplishments include International School Award sponsored by the British Council, U.K., First Place at the CBSE Regional Level Science Exhibition, Wonderla Environment and Energy Conservation Award and many more. KVGIPS is known for academic excellence, child centred activity based education and world class infrastructure.
Founder student Master Mourya R Kurunji lighting the Lamp in the presence of the founder President Dr. Kurunji Venkatramana Gowda flanked by General Secretary Dr.Renuka Prasad K.V and former Town Panchayath President Mr. N.A Ramachandra Gowda.
K.V.G. International Public School is the brainchild of Dr.Renuka Prasad K. V and Dr. Jyothi R. Prasad. The school works under a deliberate process towards full development of a child’s potential in academics, sports, art and leadership requirements for a sound future. The school curriculum is designed keeping in view of the complete growth and development of the children with child centred approach.

about

KVG International Public School started in the year 2008 with a few students and has a strength of over 421 students. It offers programmes from LKG upto 10th standard and is affiliated to the CBSE . with its innovative treatment and distinctive facilities and features, KVGIPS has come to be an acknowledged Academy of Excellence in the sphere of education today.

mission

In a safe peaceful, disciplined and intellectually challenging environment, all KVGIPS students shall have ample opportunity to :
‐ Fulfill their academic, cultural, sporting and social potential.
‐ Achieve the best possible academic result.
‐ Acquire habits of curiosity, reflection, mental flexibility, self discipline, industry and independence.
‐Maintain openness of mind, dignity of conduct and mutual respect in the face of racial, economical, cultural, religious and linguistic diversity.
‐ Develop confident understanding of teir role in an international society.

Principal Message

Education has always been a cornerstone of society, the pillar on which the bulwark of human existence rests, the leading light as mankind made his way in quest of the unknown. While the art and science of education may have undergone changes since the time of yore, every educationist strives to instill in every student a sense of virtue and wisdom, sensitivity to the needs of others, the skills, the attitudes and habits that would help them explore and excel, a quest for knowledge and the power of action. All of these would propel each one to an all important goal – Excellence.
At KVG International Public School, it is our endeavour to delve deeper into the true purpose of education by giving our students opportunities and an environment that is positive, inspiring, challenging and stimulating. We want each one of our students to follow their bliss, blaze new trails and open new doors. I am proud of the parents and teachers who care, nurture and reassure the children.
Scientists say that as per aerodynamics, the honey bee cannot fly. Its wings are not proportionate to the shape and size of its body and so scientifically speaking it is not possible for the honey bee to fly. But we know that the honey bee flies effortlessly. This is simply because the bee does not know the above fact and is so conditioned by it. The bee is aware of only one thing that it can and is sure to fly, and so it does. Keeping the very principle in mind we in the school endeavor to motivate our budding talents to persevere like the honey bee, and achieve success.
I assure that KVGIPS will always remain the foremost temple of learning, where there are “equal opportunities to all so that they rise to their fullest potential”.


With warm regards
Mr. Arun Kumar. S
Principal
